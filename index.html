What is Eve.Ai? 🌼

Eve.Ai 🧘‍♀️ is an AI-powered mental wellness companion designed to help individuals track their mood, reflect on their feelings, and receive personalized wellness tips and Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) strategies. This virtual companion is built on Google Gemini AI for generating compassionate and supportive responses, and ChromaDB for retrieving relevant wellness tips based on the user’s emotional state.

Key Features of Eve.Ai 🌟:

  • Mood Classification: Using a simple journal entry, Eve.Ai analyzes the user’s mood and provides insightful feedback. 🌈
  • Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG): Based on the user’s mood, Eve.Ai fetches relevant wellness tips, like breathing exercises or gratitude practices. 🍃
  • Personalized Support: Combines mood analysis with specific actions to guide users. 🫶
  • Weekly Mood Trend Visualization: Tracks emotional journeys, showing progress visually. 📈

The Power of AI in Mental Health 🌱

At its core, Eve.Ai leverages Google Gemini AI to generate responses that feel supportive and empathetic. These AI models use few-shot prompting, generating highly relevant content with minimal training data. Behind the Scenes: How Eve.Ai Works 🛠️

1. User Input 📝:

Start by writing a short journal entry about your feelings.

2. Mood Analysis 💭:

Sentiment analysis detects your emotional tone (e.g., anxious, stressed, calm).

3. Tip Retrieval 🌿:

Based on your mood, Eve.Ai fetches targeted tips from the wellness database.

4. Supportive Response 🤗:

Generates a personalized reply packed with mindfulness techniques or CBT strategies.

5. Weekly Mood Tracking 📊:

Visualizes your mood patterns to promote emotional self-awareness over time.

Tech Stack Behind Eve.Ai 🧠💡

  • Google Gemini AI: Generates empathetic, personalized responses.
  • ChromaDB: Semantic search database for finding relevant wellness content.
  • Visualization: Python libraries like matplotlib for emotional trend graphs. 🎨
